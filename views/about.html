Chessboard

About

Chessboard.io is simple & fastest multiplayer chess game. You can resume your chess game any time / any day later, Chessboard.io remembers your board positions for lifetime along with history of all the moves.

Chessboard.io is in very early stage, if you find any issues please report it here. Also if you've any suggestions please report at the same place.

Chessboard.io is made by Abhinay Omkar (@abhiomkar). And it is Open Source at Github.
